Potato is an award-winning digital product development studio, which develops purposeful and effective digital products and services that have a positive impact on people and the world around them.

We work with a wide variety of clients across multiple sectors including Google, Mozilla, Royal Bank of Scotland, the BBC, Amazon Alexa and Cambridge Audio.

We’re also an academy for product development talent, with the aim of helping to stop time and energy being wasted while creating happier teams and stakeholders.

Founded in 2010, we are now 90 people strong across our studios in London and San Francisco. We are WPP’s lead agency for Voice for its Global Centre of Excellence.

Potato is part of the AKQA network.
