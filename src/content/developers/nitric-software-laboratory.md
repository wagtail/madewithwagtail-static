Nitric is a tiny python-focused software company with a presence in both Cape Town, South Africa and Porto, Portugal. We work mostly in the legal and arts technology spaces. Our flagship product LawPracticeZA is an accounting and billing system for South African lawyers. We also make ticketing, point-of-sale and management systems for arts & entertainment organisations like theatres, restaurants and galleries.
