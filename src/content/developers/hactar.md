Hactar is a modern studio of designers and technologists specialising in helping grow organisations with a social conscience. We have a Lean philosophy, a smart approach to technology and a commitment to social good. We consult, workshop, present, plan, research, design, test and build in-house — usually for digital products and services. Hactar was founded to help the underserved and the oversold, and was designed from the ground up to rethink what an agency born in the twenty-first century should be. Hactar have teams in London and Glasgow.
