Siddharth Adelkar is the Tech Editor at People's Archive of Rural India. Siddharth notices nifty tools and best practices in the tech world, and brings them home to PARI. Siddharth believes that journalists expect more than “just a website” from technologists. They need an engaged tech team that is sensitive to the geopolitical, economic and societal crisis that journalism is going through. Online journalism can be a redeeming force, but that requires building an enduring partnership between techies and journalists. One that strives to rebuild the Internet as a democratic and public space.
