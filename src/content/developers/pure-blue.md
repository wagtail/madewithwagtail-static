**About Us**

We’ve been doing business since 2006. We’ve learned a great deal along the way. That experience is what we want to bring to your organization.
We’ve worked with everyone from small garage startups to Fortune 500 companies. We’ve spent the last five years refining our process and taking a deep dive into behavioral health and health data.
We launched DataWellness in the spring of 2016 and it represents the best of our creative thinking and product development practices for dealing with incredibly complex data and privacy concerns.

**Our Technology**

We love working with Django, Vue.js, Foundation, and making our own APIs. We've been actively involved in Open Source Software since we started. We are big fans of the mobile web and content first design. We also think that the Shopify Liquid Language is the bees knees.

**Dave Merwin**

Dave has been building experiences on the web since 1998, including ecommerce tools, complete content management systems, online learning systems, games and medical data systems. Dave is passionate about coming up with the right solution to the problems that you face and doing it in a creative way. Dave is a passionate fly fisherman and sailor. Sometimes he runs.
