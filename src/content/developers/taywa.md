Taywa llc was founded 2004 in Zurich, Switzerland. Our name, Taywa is Japanese (タイワ) and translates to "dialogue". Dialoge is very important to us. It's listening to the customers and explaining our solutions well. It's also the dialogue between the website/app and the users.

We design and program websites, web applications and mobile apps for a broad clientele. It consists of small to medium companies, the public sector and cultural institutions.

We develop django projects since 2008. Our django expertise is very diverse, as we have worked with multiple django content management systems and various django apps. Wagtail is by far our favourite django cms.

We often implement custom business logic for our customers. For example shop systems with interfaces to custom accounting software or course booking with automated billing.

On the frontend we have build interfaces with vuejs, angular or jQuery for simpler cases.
