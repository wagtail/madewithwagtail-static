VillageOffice promotes new forms of work and is building a nationwide network of VillageOffice coworking spaces. As a cooperative, we build bridges between municipalities, businesses, real estate owners and coworkers. Our vision: By 2030, every person in Switzerland will reach the next coworking space within 15 minutes. This will relieve the traffic infrastructure and strengthen local businesses. And we improve the quality of life by shortening the commuting distance.
