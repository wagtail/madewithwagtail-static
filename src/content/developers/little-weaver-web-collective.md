Founded at the beginning of 2014, Little Weaver is a worker-owned collective dedicated to socially-responsible design and website development and passionate about the development of a better internet built on open-source technologies. Members of our team can be found in Washington DC, San Francisco, Boston, Seattle, Vermont, Connecticut, and Ontario.
