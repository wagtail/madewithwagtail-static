Climate change is not something that only liberal believe in - it's a surprisingly bi-partisan issue! Cross the Aisle for Climate is a non-partisan group working to identify climate-related issues that both liberals and conservatives can agree on, and work toward bi-partisan decision-making in those areas. The Be Heard section lets users send email to representatives based on zip code.
