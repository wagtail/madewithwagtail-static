NHS Health Research Authority (HRA) champion the UK as a great place to do health research.

The primary audience for the HRA website are the UK/England health and social care research community, who need to find information quickly and easily in order to receive guidance to enable them to apply for and obtain approval for their research projects. The site has passed the UK Government Digital Service (GDS) Alpha, Beta and Live assessments.
