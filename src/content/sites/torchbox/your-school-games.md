School Games is a programme run by the Youth Sport Trust (YST) that delivers more than 3 million sporting opportunities for 5-18 year olds in the UK every year. The Your School Games website encourages schools and young athletes to get involved and sign-up to the programme. The site sits in front of a Django-powered custom web app (built by Torchbox) which helps over 450 organisers to coordinate and record school games and competitions across the UK.
