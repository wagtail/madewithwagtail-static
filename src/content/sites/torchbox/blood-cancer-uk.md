Torchbox partnered with Blood Cancer UK, the UK's leading blood cancer charity, to launch a new Wagtail-powered flagship website to bring [their new brand identity](https://www.pentagram.com/work/blood-cancer-uk), improve access to information and support, drive donations and online income and improve back-office processes through automated integrations with systems like their MS Dynamics CRM.
