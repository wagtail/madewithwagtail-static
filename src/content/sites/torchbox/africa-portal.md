The Africa Portal is a research repository and an expert analysis hub on African affairs.

The site aims to increase and broaden the accessibility, availability and usage of policy research on key African themes, produced primarily by African-based research institutes. The entire repository of +5k research reports, papers and policy briefs is open access, equipped with a sophisticated search function and available for free, full-text download.
