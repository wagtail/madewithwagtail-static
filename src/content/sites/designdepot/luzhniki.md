Today Luzhniki is one of the largest sports grounds in Russia and the world. The unified architectural and park ensemble of the sports complex unites more than 80 structures and facilities, the most significant of which are the Big Sports Arena, the Small Sports Arena, the Sports Palace, the Aqua Complex, the Northern Sports Center, the Southern Sports Center, the Druzhba Sports Center, the Crystal Ice Palace, Sports town, Tennis club.
