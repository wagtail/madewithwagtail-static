myBaze.com is fashion aggregation website with around 3M products updated on a daily basis using ~70 e-commerce stores.

It's currently available to a polish market only. However we are working on including other european countries in the following 2021 year.

Site is using the following tech stack:

Wagtail; Django; ElasticSearch; Redis; Postgres; RabbitMQ; Celery; Consul; Vue (a little);

Managed by: Consul, Nomad, Vault (great products from Hashicorp).
