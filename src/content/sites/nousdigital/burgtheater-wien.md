Burgtheater Wien is the Austrian National Theater in Vienna, one of the most important German-language theaters in the world.

Their website reflects the breadth that the Burgtheater has to offer in its several venues: multiple ways to browse its program and subscriptions, a favorite function for events, live ticket availability, background information about events and casts, as well as a separate section for "Offene Burg," aimed especially at a younger audience.
