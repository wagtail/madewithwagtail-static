The website based on Django/Python conveys the tasks of the Bundestheater Holding and offers a daily updated overview of shows offered at different stages. The presentation data is taken from the central database. Wagtail, a particularly user-friendly content management system (CMS), is used. Special attention was given to a completely responsive view of the website, which takes into account the different requirements of different devices and presents itself in a user-friendly way. The site not only facilitates access to service information, but also facilitates a compact tour of the Austrian national theatres.
