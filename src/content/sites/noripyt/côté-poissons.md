_Côté poissons_ regroups three famous places in Rouen, France:

- a fishery, _la Poissonnerie des Halles_
- the restaurant _Comptoir des Halles_
- the restaurant _Seine & Quai_

This website – published in January 2018 – allows visitors to check out the menu for both restaurants as well as the reviews made by clients using Guest Suite, an online guestbook to rate restaurants & hotels and leave them a comment.

Design by [Alexandre Plu](http://ap-design.io/).
