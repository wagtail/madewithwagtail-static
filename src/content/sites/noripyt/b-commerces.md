B Commerces is an agency specialized in business real estate sale.

This site mainly allows B Commerces to publish real estate ads online. Each real estate is not only published on the site, but also automatically sent to the famous French site [Le Bon Coin](https://www.leboncoin.fr/boutique/52163/b_commerces_entreprises.htm?ca=11_s&w=3&c=8), as well as [CessionPME](https://www.cessionpme.com/).

It also serves another purpose: it allows B Commerces to store privately all technical informations on a given real estate, as well as its followup.

This website was published in October 2017.
