NoriPyt is part of a [BNI](https://www.bnifrance.fr/) group called Utopia, and created its website during September 2016. It was made again in January 2017 to show group members and activities.

The site was dropped in April 2018 because BNI introduced a new system providing a subsite for each group, including [a BNI Utopia subsite](http://bni76.fr/76---seine-maritime-76-21-bni-rouen-utopia/fr/index).
