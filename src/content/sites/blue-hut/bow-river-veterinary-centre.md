With a high proportion of four-legged tourists visiting the area the website needed to be clean and exceptionally easy to use, especially in mobile situations for emergencies. The site concentrates on providing quick access to information to help pet parents make important decisions about their furry offspring. Bow Tie Zack, the centre’s resident cat, also runs a blog on the site.
