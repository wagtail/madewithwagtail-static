El Toro is a locally owned and operated restaurant providing great food for residents and visitors to Banff National Park for over 40 years. The entire menu is preloaded into the backend and using a simple checkbox items can be displayed or removed quickly by the kitchen. Uploading photographs for menu items also allows potential diners to check out what the food looks like before they try.
