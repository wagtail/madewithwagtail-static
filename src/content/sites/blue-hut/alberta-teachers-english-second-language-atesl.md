This site operates a tiered admin system allowing content administrators access to their own Chapters sections to streamline updates, with varying levels of access within each tier. Over 1,300 existing members accounts were also migrated into the new backend and admin levels. Membership renewal is also offered online, including automatic renewal notifications. By making the admin interface as intuitive as possible content editors require minimal training to manage the content under their remit.
