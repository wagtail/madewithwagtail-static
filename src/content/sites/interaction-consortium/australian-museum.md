Australian Museum is the oldest museum in Australia with a collection encompassing both anthropology and natural history. This new website integrates a DAMS, ticketing and a CRM as well as implementing a usable, but full-featured events calendar. Our design won an award at the 2019 Museums Australasia Multimedia & Publication Design Awards.
