NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory’s cutting-edge new digital home for the lab’s missions, science news, images and videos. The headless JPL site is powered by Wagtail and Vue using Nuxt.js. The project was delivered by a fully remote multi-agency/client project team with JPL designers and engineers collaborating with Torchbox and Moore Boeck.

Tom Dyson, Torchbox co-founder and Wagtail Lead said _“We’re so proud to have worked with JPL on this project, which was carried out by a completely remote team, in the time it took Perseverance to travel 300 million miles to Mars. Wagtail isn’t rocket science, but it played a part in this extraordinary achievement, and we’re very grateful to everyone in the open source community who’s been part of it.”_
