QrCodeGo is a free QR code maker. Its free for non commercial & commercial use. This web gives you ability to generate your customized qr code by customizing shape, color and blending logo.

Our service supports generation from link, text, phone, sms, email, WIFI, VCard and Google Maps. I plan to implement some more features. This is my first Django/Wagtail project. I work on this site in my free time. Wagtail is an awesome CMS I integrated into my project. This site is multilingual, however, not everything is translated fully. It supports English, Polish, Spanish, Russian, Italian. I initially used Wagtail for description of "how generator works". At the moment there are only two languages : English and Polish. But I plan to develop much more. Of course depends on my free time...

Thanks for ability to learn and for wagtail!
