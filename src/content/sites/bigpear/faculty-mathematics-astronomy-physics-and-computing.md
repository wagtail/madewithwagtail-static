With the everlasting commitment to expand the academic offering, to contribute towards the technological development that the society requires. The Faculty of Mathematics, Astronomy, Physics, and Computing (FaMAF), belonging to the  National University of Cordoba (UNC), provides a broad curriculum from the core discipline of Mathematics, Astronomy, Physics and Computer Science to Criminology, Neuroscience and High Performance Computing, to name a few.
