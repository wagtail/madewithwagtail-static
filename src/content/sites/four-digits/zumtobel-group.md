Improve the quality of life through light. The right light. At the right time. In the right place.

Zumtobel Group is one of the largest international players in the field of lighting solutions.

Together with the [Zumtobel Group](https://z.lighting/) and design studio [Anton & Irene](https://antonandirene.com/), [Four Digits](https://fourdigits.nl) have worked on the digital platform in which the various brand websites can be managed in one complete solution using Wagtail CMS as its main engine.

A large part of the website is for case studies: the largest and most inspiring locations where Zumtobel Group has supplied their products. The Zumtobel Group has placed its lamps in world-famous churches in Italy, various museums and the most famous stadiums of major European football clubs.
