Stoffenmanager® is a knowledge-based platform aimed at reducing exposure risks to hazardous substances and biological agents in the workplace. They offer a sustainable and simple solution that helps organisations meet regulatory requirements and to be responsible employers by creating a safe workplace.. They do this by means of consultancy, training courses and the scientifically validated tool, Stoffenmanager® version 8.
