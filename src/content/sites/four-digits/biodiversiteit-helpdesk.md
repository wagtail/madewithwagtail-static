The BiodiversiteitHelpdesk is the place where anyone can receive a scientifically substantiated answer to their biodiversity related questions.

Many people are looking for trustworthy and easy to understand information about biodiversity. What exactly is it, what is the social impact of loss of biodiversity, and more. Finding the answers to these questions on the internet isn't that easy. How do you know that what you are reading is even scientifically correct?

The BiodiversiteitHelpdesk wants to help with that. They connect the questions about biodiversity with the right experts. This way they share scientific information about biodiversity in an understandable way for the public. They hope to start a dialogue between scientists and citizens. They have a network of experts that can answers questions.
