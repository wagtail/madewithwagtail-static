CWI conducts pioneering research in mathematics and computer science and transfers new knowledge to society and business. We focus on 4 areas of fundamental research: Algorithms, Data & Intelligent systems, Cryptography & Security and Quantum Computing. CWI works closely with Dutch universities through semester programmes, joint research programs and an active exchange of scientific talent.
