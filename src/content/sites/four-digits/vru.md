The Veiligheidsregio Utrecht is a cooperative of and for all 26 Utrecht municipalities in the province of Utrecht. Together with our partners, we ensure the safety of all people living and staying in the region every day. We do that with our dispatchers at the 112 alarm center, our firefighters and our colleagues from risk management, crisis management, the medical assistance organisation in the region (GHOR) and the support services.
