Van Hall Larenstein University of Applied Sciences (HVHL) is a renowned educational institution focused on offering practice-oriented courses in the field of sustainability, nature and the environment. The new website, developed by Four Digits, is designed to support the college's international ambitions and provides comprehensive information on the various courses and services offered by HVHL. An important aspect of this website is its multilingualism. HVHL targets a broad, international audience, which is why the site is available in Dutch, German and English. This makes it easier for international students and stakeholders to find and understand information, contributing to the attractiveness of the college to a global audience.

The website provides comprehensive information on the various courses offered by HVHL. Visitors can find detailed descriptions of both undergraduate and graduate programmes, including admission requirements, curriculum details and career opportunities. This section has been carefully designed to help users make informed decisions about their education and future. The website offers integration with Microsoft Dynamics via forms. This integration ensures automated and streamlined data transfer between the website and HVHL's internal systems. It integrates with Surfconext for secure and user-friendly authentication. This enables students and staff to access various online services and applications with a single login, improving the user experience and ensuring the security of sensitive data. We ensured that the site complies with the latest web standards, enhancing usability and accessibility.

The choice of Wagtail CMS allows HVHL to easily manage and update content, thanks to its intuitive and user-friendly interface and underlying capabilities using the Django Framework and Python. Four Digits has delivered a modern, responsive and accessible website that complies with the latest web standards, ensuring an optimal user experience on all devices. Van Hall Larenstein University of Applied Sciences' new website is an excellent tool that helps the educational institution achieve its international goals and support students worldwide. With its multilingual design, advanced integrations and user-friendly interface, the website provides everything needed to effectively communicate HVHL's message and services to a wide audience.
