RijnIJssel educates their students sustainably: for a place in the current job market and that of the future, and for active participation in society. They share the interest that their students have in a subject or help them discover it, so that they’re well prepared for the future.

With over 12 thousand students, RijnIJssel is the largest school for secondary vocational education in Gelderland. They have over 20 locations in Arnhem, Wageningen and surroundings. They offer a wide range of vocational educations. They let their students study at school as well as in the work field. Theory and practice are completely integrated and go hand in hand. One of the things they value, is for their education to be adapted to the work field as well as the needs of their students.
