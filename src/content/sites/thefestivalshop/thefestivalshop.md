TheFestival.Shop is a free online magazine which features anything and everything related to music festivals. Founded in 2017, we are strong believers in the pure and awesome experiences that only music festivals provide. They can bring people from all ages and walks of life together for a common cause, whether it be the love of music, a chance to meet old friends or make new ones, or simply just to wind back have fun. And who doesn't like fun? Every day we hunt through all corners of the web to find some of the most awesome products which we believe would bring value to fellow festival goers. Our philosophy is simple: find awesome stuff and list it here.
