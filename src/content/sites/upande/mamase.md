The MaMaSe Sustainable Water Initiative website is used to highlight the efforts being undertaken by various partners to conserve the Mau Mara Serengeti water basin. It is aimed at improving water safety and security in the Mara River Basin to support structural poverty reduction, sustainable economic growth and conservation of the basin’s ecosystems.
