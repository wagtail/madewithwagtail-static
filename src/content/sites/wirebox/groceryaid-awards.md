GroceryAid currently uses Wagtail for their main website but needed a second site to advertise and promote their yearly awards programme to celebrate the backing they receive from the retail industry. In order to fulfil their requirement, we worked closely with the marketing team to produce a solution and designs that would ensure that the management of the site remains simple and easy, but stands out to users. Utilising Wagtail's multi-site functionality we developed the GroceryAid Awards website, which sits on GroceryAid's domain. Developing the site in this way allowed us to ensure that the marketing team could continue to easily manage the content on both sites without having to utilise another CMS. The Awards site is split between 3 tiers, Gold, Silver & Bronze in order to rank the participants that completely backed GroceryAid in 2018. There is also a company search feature that you can use to view the award you won, making it quick and easy for participants to find out if they had won an award.
