This is my personal website where I promote my services as a freelance SEO consultant in Wellington.

It was made with Wagtail to enable me to easily publish blog posts, create new landing pages, tweak page content, and track form submissions.

As a Wagtail newbie, I've been in awe at how flexible it is as a Django CMS, and how easy it is to jump right into with limited web development experience.

I've written an article here about my personal experience creating this website as a non-developer: <https://riaparish.co.nz/blog/my-first-wagtail-site/>.
