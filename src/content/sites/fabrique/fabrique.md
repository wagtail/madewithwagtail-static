Fabrique is a strategic design agency in the Netherlands.

This website showcases our projects, services and expertise, and our studios, vacancies and people.

After some experience with Wagtail for our clients, we've overhauled our own website to use Wagtail in early 2017, to the extreme delight of our own communication department.

For the Dutch version of our site, visit [fabrique.nl](https://fabrique.nl), which is managed from the same admin (language of the site is determined by the domain name).
