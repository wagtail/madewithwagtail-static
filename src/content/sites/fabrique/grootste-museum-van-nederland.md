Website for Grootste Museum van Nederland, literally the Largest Museum of the Netherlands, but known in English as Dutch Museum Churches.

Dutch Museum Churches is an initiative of Museum Catharijne Convent, and it's a cooperation between several churches all over the Netherlands. The idea is that all these churches with all there splendor, history, art and stories, together form the Largest Museum of the Netherlands.
