The Hermitage Amsterdam is a branch of the Hermitage Museum in Saint Peterburg, Russia.

The museum houses two permanent presentations and temporary exhibitions usually scheduled for six-months.

The site provides an overview of all the activities. It is very block based, opening a block will in fact load a partial page that can be managed as a special 'Inline Page' in Wagtail.
