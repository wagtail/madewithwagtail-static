Fabrique Public & Industrial Design is a sister company of Fabrique, focusing more on the real world rather than the digital one. Their varied portfolio ranges from bus shelters, interior design for public buildings to glass roof awnings and lighting solutions.

After Fabrique's main website was converted to Wagtail in the beginning of 2017, Fabrique3D's website followed late 2017. It uses the same code base, but runs as a separate installation. This provided some interesting challenges, as the sites are similar in structure and in style, but not quite identical.

Visit the Dutch version of the website at [www.fabrique3d.nl](https://www.fabrique3d.nl)
