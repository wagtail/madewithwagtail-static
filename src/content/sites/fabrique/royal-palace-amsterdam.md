Website for the Royal Palace on the Dam Square in Amsterdam. Provides visitor information and some historical background information. Allows you to book tickets and tours.

Behind the scenes, there is a booking system built with Wagtail Modeladmin, an integration with GlobalTicket for tickets and a management console for the screens in the palace and an application to manage artworks that are on loan.

Everything is available in Dutch and English and the most important information in 6 more languages.
