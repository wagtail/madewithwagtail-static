The Sitka Technology Group is a team of experienced developers, project managers and consultants committed to successful outcomes in complex natural resources programs. Sitka provides knowledge infrastructures for conservation, restoration, and sustainable development. They specialize in supporting clients who work in medium-to-large-scale environments, whether a watershed or a metropolitan area.
