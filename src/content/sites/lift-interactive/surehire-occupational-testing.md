SureHire provides health testing, fitness to work, and other health services to individuals and employers across Canada. We've worked with SureHire for years, and as their business has grown, the requirements for their website have changed.

When it was time to complete a redesign, we chose to use Wagtail for it's flexibility. The site is designed to reflect updates to the brand and company and to present clearly the breadth of services that SureHire offers. We worked to create clear calls to action throughout the site in an effort to drive increased lead activity.
