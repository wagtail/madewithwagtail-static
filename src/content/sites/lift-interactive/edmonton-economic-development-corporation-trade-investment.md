A division of Edmonton Economic Development Corporation, EEDC’s Trade & Investment team supports companies in the Edmonton Metro Region who are taking a risk in new markets by making the leap from local to global. They also work to attract new business from around the world to invest in Edmonton, assisting with a smooth transition into our regional market.

We built the site around funnelling EEDC's two distinct audiences into either "Invest" or "Export," where they can find specific data and information to support their next steps.

Inspiring articles on each side tell the stories of companies making a new home in or growing outside of Edmonton. We built a robust and flexible storytelling section which allows EEDC team members to easily create inspiring stories with dynamic layouts.
