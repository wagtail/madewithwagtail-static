In 2012, a volunteer task force was brought together to answer a question, ‘What is Edmonton all about?’

Working with the task force, we fleshed out their ideas and built a platform that allows users to post projects, big or small, in order to demonstrate what they are making (or have made) in Edmonton. Project profiles have flexible action buttons that help raise funds, round up volunteers or simply generate buzz.
