The Organic Box connects you with local producers who are growing and crafting organic food and eco-friendly products. Sign up for a box, and you’ll receive farm-fresh produce, free-range meats, and more—straight to your door.

The pages and blog sections were created with Wagtail and integrated into the rest of the application.
