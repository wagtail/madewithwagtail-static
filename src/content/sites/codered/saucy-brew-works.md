Brewery in Cleveland Ohio serving craft beer and amazing pizza.

For Saucy Brew Works, respect for community means being responsible with our resources. The brewery’s systems reduce water. Fresh Fork Market uses our spent grains to feed their animals. We in turn will buy their meat — and the world goes round. Respect also means supporting education. That’s why we support Breakthrough Schools, a top performing community school that takes a different approach to education. Just like we take a different approach to beer.
