New website of the Swiss association for leading practitioners in the Public Health area. Built with Wagtail and Puput with a Bootstrap theme, we migrated hundreds of bilingual pages of content and supported a small team of editors in getting a hang of the platform. The site is hosted in a cloud VPS using Ansible, Docker and lots of fine tea.
