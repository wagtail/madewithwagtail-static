Parrano, known for the tasty cheeses, asked Lukkien to develop a creative concept that serves as the basis for their brand positioning and all online communications. Because of Parrano’s Italian roots, we integrated an Italian twist into the new website. To realize this, our Digital, Photography & Graphic Design and Audiovisual departments joined forces.

Lukkien Digital developed the new website. For the Parrano recipes, Lukkien Audiovisual made videos which explain how to create the perfect dishes. Photography & Graphic Design was responsible for the beautiful portrayal of the dishes.

The result is that Parrano can communicate with a new and clear brand positioning.
