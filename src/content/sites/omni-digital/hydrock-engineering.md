Hydrock is a multi-disciplinary engineering design consultancy. They wanted a new site with an inspiring and industry-leading design, and required an easy-to-use and streamlined backend editing experience.

Wagtail has enabled editors to create complex and engaging content using the blocks framework, while simultaneously allowing for an elegant & simple site structure.

One of the aspects of this system is a GDPR compliant form builder, which will be used to execute their data protection impact assessments, and audit their existing databases of private data. The form builder allows Hydrock admins to produce forms with any number of fields, and serve them on any page of the site. The form builder integrates seamlessly with the admins' existing workflow, and all form data submitted is stored encrypted in the database.
