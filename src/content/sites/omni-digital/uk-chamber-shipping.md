The UK Chamber of Shipping works with policy makers and international organisations to champion and protect the UK's shipping industry.

As part of a drive to increase openness and transparency, the Chamber needed the ability to create rich, engaging and beautiful content with fine-grained control of page publishing to allow more access to data for interested parties and the public, without jeopardising sensitive data.

The Chamber's new Wagtail site allows them unlimited flexibility in content creation thanks to the beautiful StreamField editor, and and intuitive publishing system which allows information about all Chamber activity to be accessed by members of the public.
