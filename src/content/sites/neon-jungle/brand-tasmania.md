The Brand Tasmania showcase website was designed by The20 and built in partnership by Neon Jungle. This beautiful website features an array of rich video content that takes audiences on a journey through the stories of real Tasmanians doing great things.

Functionally, the site provides Tasmanians, interstate and overseas audiences with everything they need to know about living, working, visiting, trading and growing in this state.

We are so proud of this project and look forward to continuing our work with The20 and the Brand Tasmania team to keep building upon the Tasmanian website for years to come.
