The Multiple Sclerosis Research Flagship is a Flagship program of the University of Tasmania, College of Health and Medicine and was established from a long history of multiple sclerosis (MS) research at Menzies Institute for Medical Research.

The MS Research Flagship team wanted to create a website that felt inclusive, approachable and informative, catering to their two audiences – those within the MS community and those involved in MS research.

The result is a simple, content-rich website that welcomes users to discover the support, patient and family resources and the latest in life-changing research. The website serves a vital purpose for those living with or supporting someone with MS, and we’re proud to work with this incredibly important organisation.
