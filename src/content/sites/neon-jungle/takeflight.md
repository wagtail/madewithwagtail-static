Takeflight are a bunch of highly skilled developers and designers who create innovative and engaging websites and apps.

When developing our own website with Wagtail, we designed a site that worked as a portfolio of our work and a resource to contact and view our team. The content was structured using sharp abstract shapes matching with our branding and clearly displayed text. An intercom chat box was also added for clients to interact more easily with us.
