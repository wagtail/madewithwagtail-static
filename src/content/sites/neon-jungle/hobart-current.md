Hobart Current is an innovative long-term partnership between the City of Hobart and the Tasmanian Museum and Art Gallery, which replaces the 27-year-old City of Hobart Art Prize. It is a major biennial program that will nurture and showcase contemporary artists working across different media, which may include visual art, performance, music, film, design, and literature.

Professional, creative, informative - the Hobart Current website surfaces important information and a timeline of events through mixed media content blocks that embody the contemporary aesthetic of the festival brand.

Our team are heavily involved in the local arts scene in Tasmania and we thoroughly enjoyed creating a platform for Hobart Current to engage with their community and bring awareness to this fantastic program.
