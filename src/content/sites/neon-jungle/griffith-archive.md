The Griffith Archive is a repository for historical artefacts relating to Griffith University since its concept and inception. The Archive records Griffith’s story by collecting and digitally/physically preserving relevant documents, photographs, oral histories, memorabilia and other artefacts. It also details significant aspects of the organisation’s history and changes through the years.
