The Tasmanian Labor Party needed a better platform for accepting donations, membership payments and selling merchandise than the offsite payment processor they had been using. We built a Wagtail site to sit alongside their current WordPress website with custom UX design to make it as easy as possible for users to support the party.

Later, we added a custom survey feature, to allow the creation, running and analysis of multi-part surveys.
