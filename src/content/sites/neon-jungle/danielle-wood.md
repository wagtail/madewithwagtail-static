A versatile wordsmith, Danielle Wood writes fiction, non-fiction, essays and prose poetry, and has a growing stable of _noms-de-plume_, under which she also writes children's fiction and romantic comedies.

We designed and developed Danielle Wood's website, as well as the subsidiary websites for her pseudonym works. We made use of Wagtail's multi-tenant site building capabilities to provide Danielle a simple way to manage all her websites in one place.
