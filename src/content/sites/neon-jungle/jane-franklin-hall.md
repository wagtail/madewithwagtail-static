Jane Franklin Hall is a residential college for students studying at the University of Tasmania. They are the premier college experience in Hobart, and produce the future leaders, innovators and thinkers of society.

Jane approached us in 2018 to refresh their brand and digital presence.

As part of the refresh, we created a range of digital and print products, including a new website, annual report templates, letterheads, business cards, and banners. Since launch, the rebrand has revitalised Jane’s digital and physical presence, helping to sell the vast benefits of living at Jane.
