Bitlink is a technology education company based in Launceston, Tasmania. For the last decade, the Bitlink team have been dedicated to helping young people learn more about technology. By working directly with teachers and students, they build kits, lessons and activities that students love and that make teachers lives easier.

We worked with Bitlink on the design and development of their website, which is the primary learning platform used alongside their technology kits.

With a target audience split between students, teachers and government, we faced the challenge of coming up with a look and feel that balanced fun and approachability with technical expertise and professionalism.
