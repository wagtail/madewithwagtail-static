The Diemen Awards celebrates Tasmania's best and brightest talent in commercial creativity.

One of Australia’s leading creative industry awards, the Diemen Awards evolved out of the Printing Industries Craftsmanship Awards (PICA) which showcased Tasmania's craft and print culture for over two decades. Since 2014 it’s been their goal to celebrate Tasmania’s talent in commercial creativity, including design, advertising, print and digital communications.

We knew the Diemen Awards new website had to be pretty special – after all, it's main audience would be some of the most talented creatives in the state!

We loved creating this site, and took extra care in applying the finishing touches that would be make it shine. With a hint of gold shimmer on link hovers, a transforming trophy on the homepage scroll and some seamless transitions on collapsable content blocks, this is definitely one of our proudest Wagtail developments!
