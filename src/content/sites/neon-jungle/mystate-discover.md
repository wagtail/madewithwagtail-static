Takeflight collaborated with MyState Bank to develop a microsite called ‘Discover’. The microsite is used as a tool for MyState customers to discover more about their financial goals, and how to achieve them.

Using the MyState brand as the basis for the microsite design, the step-by-step selection and swift process merge to create an easy to use tool that benefits the user and allows MyState to learn more about the desires and needs of their customers.
