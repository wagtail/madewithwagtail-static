Based in Hobart, Tasmania, the team at Bold Law operate a streamlined practice that allows them to focus on providing a great experience and achieving outstanding results without excessive fees.

Neon Jungle worked with Bold Law to create a sophisticated, approachable website that deviates from the stuffy, corporate legal status quo. Bold by name and by nature, this website caters directly to their state-based audience segments through uniquely-crafted landing pages. Striking content blocks and parallax scrolling make Bold Law's website stand out from the crowd.
