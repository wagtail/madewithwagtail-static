CoreMarine are mashing industry-leading software together with ocean construction and we love what they're about.

After undertaking their bold rebrand, we were tasked with the design and development of their new website. The main goal? Standing out amongst the sea of boring ocean-themed websites of their competitors (pun intended). We love the mega menu and the light touches of animation that really bring the design to life within the browser.

We used Wagtail Grapple to set up a GraphQL API for the content of the website. The frontend is hosted on Vercel, with Lottie being utilised for animations and transitions.
