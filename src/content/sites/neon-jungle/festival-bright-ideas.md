Festival of Bright Ideas is a community based educational festival that engages children in hands-on science based activities and promotes careers in scientific careers as important, achievable and locally relevant.

Takeflight worked alongside the festival producers to develop a brand and website that matched the level of engagement, playfulness and forward thinking characteristics of the festival. As you scroll through the website, there is a flow through each section of content and imagery. The site is a balance of detailed and block colours, with graphics that give impressions of a range of depths – mirroring the range of sciences that are on display in the festival.
