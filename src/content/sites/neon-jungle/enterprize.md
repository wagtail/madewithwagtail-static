Enterprize is Tasmania’s startup space –offering a supportive environment that encourages businesses to grow and thrive on their own. They recently renovated their spaces and approached Neon Jungle to design a new brand and website to match their new, modern coworking space.

Neon Jungle designed an innovative and relatable brand that embraced the Enterprize story, and reflects the opportunities that Enterprize is now able to offer its members. The brand easily carries across all marketing collateral in a professional and eyecatching way.

The Enterprize website is a hub of information that showcases community events, workshops and encourages new members to sign up to the space. For existing members, the Enterprize website offers an easy access portal and directory of current members to encourage networking and community.

We are proud of the brand and website we created for Enterprize and look forward to working with them further in the future
