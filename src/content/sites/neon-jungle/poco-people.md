Poco People are a Hobart based graphic design firm specialising in print based design. They have won many awards for their print design and wanted a website that doubled as an online portfolio for prospective clients.

Takeflight worked alongside Poco People to develop their designs and created a simple and striking site. With a clever use of imagery, clear navigation and an effective slideshow of their portfolio content, the Poco People website is a perfect blend of print and digital.
