Earthlings are handcrafted, sustainable, reusable cups made in Tasmania.

Neon Jungle designed an e-commerce website as a platform to sell Earthlings that features an illustration to showcase the elements from the earth from which the cups are made. The site design is intentionally simple with a focus on the cups that are for sale. Inspired by high-end fashion and sustainable brands, the Earthling site reflects the individualism of each cup.
