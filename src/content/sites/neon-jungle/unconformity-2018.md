The Unconformity is an arts festival inspired by a rare geological unconformity and the remarkable cultural paradoxes of Queenstown, a small mining community in Tasmania's wild West Coast.

Neon Jungle are the strategic and digital partner of The Unconformity, working together to bring a comprehensive festival site to life, as well as a year-round digital experience that highlights all of the amazing projects that exist in this community outside of the festival.

We are passionate supporters of the arts industry, and value our ongoing relationship with The Unconformity!
