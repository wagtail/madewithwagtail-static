Rummin are a Tasmanian based film and digital media production company specialising in cinematic documentary, narrative video and digital media projects.

We designed their logo and branding - then developed this simple website to match. In their brief they wanted each of the project pages to be a suitable main page for the project so we let their beautiful content take over and kept the design as minimal as possible.
