The Tassie Tourism Ambassador Program has been created specifically for the Tasmanian Tourism industry as a way to equip tourism and hospitality staff and interested locals with the skills to offer a great visitor experience and local knowledge to tell the story of the region.

We designed the Tassie Tourism Ambassador website to feel fun, interactive and approachable. The course is broken up into 4 modules that users can move through at their own page, with the option to complete additional location-based modules for a more broad training experience. Each module is made up of video content, followed by a series of quizzes that users must complete correctly to move forward. We love the bright, retro design of the Tassie Tourism Ambassador website and have enjoyed contributing to a valuable tourism initiative within our state.
