The Link operates out of Hobart, Tasmania, and provides free and confidential health and wellbeing services for young people aged 12-25.

We designed and developed a new website for The Link, to make gaining information about the service more available to young people. The new design is playful and bright and clear in its navigation and contact details.

In 2020, we revisited their website to launch This Way // That Way - a blog designed to feature resources, collaborations and useful information for a 12-24 year old audience on all things sex, relationships and wellbeing.
