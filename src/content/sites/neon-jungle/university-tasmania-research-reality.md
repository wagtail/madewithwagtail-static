The University of Tasmania needed to revamp the way they were spreading the word about the amazing research taking place on the tiny island at the bottom of the world.

The existing CMS didn't allow the flexibility to produce rich, beautiful articles without requiring manual HTML editing - leaving the options of either centralising editing or dramatically simplifying the layout. Our solution, built in Wagtail, provides an interface where anyone can create truly engaging content quickly and easily.
