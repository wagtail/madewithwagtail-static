Working Chance were looking for a an update across everything from their brand to print and website. They didn’t want to focus on being seen as a charity, but more as a business that focused on getting candidates into the right organisations.

The re-launched website had a refreshed design language, a cleaner and more intuitive user experience, and allowed the Working Chance team and users to work more efficiently - freeing people up to do people stuff, and letting computers get on with computer stuff.
