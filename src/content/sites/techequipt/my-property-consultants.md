My Property Consultants were a very brand conscious company that had an online presence that didn't meet the brand. The ability to interface with their other systems and provide a great user experience to their internal staff were important factors in the choice to use Wagtail CMS.

TechEquipt worked with MyPropertyConsultants to design, develop, host and provide ongoing management, maintenance and new features - ensuring a top class website in a very appearance driven industry.
