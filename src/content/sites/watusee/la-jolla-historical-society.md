The La Jolla Historical Society (LJHS) had a very old and proprietary PHP site. Watusee and Design Aranda teamed up to create a more contemporary and easy-to-use site both for themselves and their constituents. This responsive site uses Foundation 6 and with a Shopify integration for e-commerce.
