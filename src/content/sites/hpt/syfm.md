Stephen Yardley Furniture Makers approached us to complete a full design and rebuild of their website.
The company works with a very exclusive clientele and travel the world designing, building and installing bespoke interiors.

It was important for the client that they had great image management in their CMS along with a simple clean interface and to minimise the likelyhood of mistakes or miss-configurations.

We created a wagtail solution with a restriced user interface and publishing permissions hierachy that allowed the client confidence for staff to work on the site with minimal risk of embaressing mistakes making it to live pages.
