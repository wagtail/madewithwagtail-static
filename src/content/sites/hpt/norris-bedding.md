We were approached by this historic London bed manufacturer to build them a new website.
It was imperative that the site looked good whilst also being very easy to manage. The client had previous bad experience with Wordpress and was keen to improve usability to avoid repeated costs when wanting to update site content.

We build the site using Wagtail and made use of the Streamfield functionality to enable the client more flexibility to build pages from scratch, rather than be restricted to fixed content boxes as in previous sites they had comissioned.
