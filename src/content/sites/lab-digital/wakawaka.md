WakaWaka is a global social enterprise that develops and sells portable solar products for the more than a billion people living without access to electricity and the billions seeking renewable energy solutions. WakaWaka means “shine bright” in Swahili. For every WakaWaka purchased, a solar light is donated to a family living without access to electricity. Having energy is a necessity for everyone, but it’s not available for everyone. WakaWaka makes it possible to have access to energy anytime at anyplace. With proud Lab Digital is part of the development and maintenance of this wagtail site (combined with Django Oscar). In a short period of time Lab Digital expanded the website for WakaWaka and created completed a re-design.
