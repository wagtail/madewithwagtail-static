Krepel is a family business that will exist 150 years in 2020. The eighth generation is now at the helm of the company in which development has always been an important pillar. It all started with a copper mill, which eventually fell out of favor. At that time, opportunities in the market were immediately anticipated and Krepel developed into a manufacturer of cigar boxes. This degree of agility is in the DNA of the Krepel family and therefore in the DNA of the family business.
