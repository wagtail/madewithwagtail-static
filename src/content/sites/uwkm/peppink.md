Peppink Mills; the specialist in milling, grinding, pulverizing and crushing. Peppink Mills, based in the Netherlands, supplies various types of mills, such as beater cross mills, hammer mills, pen mills and grinders. Peppink Mills BV supplies mills in different sizes, so there is always a Peppink mill suitable for your situation. Peppink Mills is also able to supply ATEX certified mills for both ATEX zone 22 and ATEX zone 21. ATEX standardization means that the mills are suitable for situations with explosion risks, making Peppink mills explosion-proof against dust explosions, among other things.
