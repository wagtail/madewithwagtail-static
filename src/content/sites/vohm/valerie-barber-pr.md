We created a new, fully content manageable Wagtail website for this London based music PR company. Articles and artist profiles allow them to publish and archive press releases for the varied roster of classical artists and orchestras they represent.

Pages, artists and articles each have their own page types, with pre-defined, reorderable fields and a wide choice of stream fields allowing them to create varied and interesting content layouts from preset template options.
