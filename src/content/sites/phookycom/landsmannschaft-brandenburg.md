Our association is a fencing student fraternity which has now been in existence for almost 139 years. The purpose of this fraternity or reason for membership is only secondarily the preservation of student traditions (such as wearing colours and fencing). First and foremost it is about celebrating and having fun with each other and with many other students in Germany.
