The UK Association for Science and Discovery Centres (ASDC) brings together over 60 of the UK's major science engagement organisations in the UK. The site details the numerous national science projects, specifically STEM engagement programmes which the organisation devises and delivers. In addition it includes a resource to locate and contact science centres across the UK, details of their events and training programmes, national impact reports and a resource centre for science engagement.
