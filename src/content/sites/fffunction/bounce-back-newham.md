A site for Bounce Back, a resilience programme for young people living in the London borough of Newham. They provide help and support for young people to help them overcome the challenges, difficulties and setbacks in their lives.

The site is an educational resource platform with a voice which is engaging and respectful to its audience. Providing guides for setting and achieving goals, having healthy relationships, lifestyle, healthy eating and wellbeing it includes a user-centred resource search section tailored for its younger target site users.
