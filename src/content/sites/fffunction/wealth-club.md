A site for UK tax-efficient investment broker Wealth Club. The company provides investment products such as venture capital trusts, EIS (enterprise investment schemes) and Inheritance Tax portfolios.

The site provides many downloadable resources such as investment notes and application forms. To comply with financial regulations users must self-certify that they qualify to receive information on such investments, which carry higher risks and are less easily sold than mainstream stocks and shares.
