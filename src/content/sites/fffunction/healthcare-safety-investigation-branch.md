A site for the Healthcare Safety Investigation Branch (HSIB) - an independent safety investigation, guidance and advisory organisation based in the UK. By investigating safety incidents each year HSIB provide meaningful safety recommendations, sharing findings and insights across the whole of the healthcare system for the benefit of everyone who is cared for by it and works in it.

The site provides details of the organisation's safety investigation process, advice and support for patients and families, for healthcare staff and healthcare organisations as well as investigation reports.
