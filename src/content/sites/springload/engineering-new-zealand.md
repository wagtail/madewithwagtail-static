Engineering New Zealand is a non-profit membership organisation dedicated to promoting the interests of engineers by having a voice on topical issues.

New Zealand’s professional body for engineers since 1914, Engineering New Zealand creates a community of learning, collaboration, and support for engineers from all disciplines throughout the lifespan of their careers.
