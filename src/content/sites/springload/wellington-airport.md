Wellington International Airport is the third busiest airport of New Zealand, helping take more than 6 million passengers direct to 25 destinations around the world.
They are passionate about recognising the work of volunteers and community groups, so in conjunction with Wellington Community Trust and local council, they celebrate their valuable contribution with the Wellington Airport Regional Community Awards.
