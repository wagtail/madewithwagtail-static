LIC is a herd-improvement and agri-technology co-operative that has been improving the lives of New Zealand farmers for more than 100 years.

Their commitment to research and development keeps LIC at the leading edge of the dairy farming industry. As a result New Zealand herds have some of the highest rates of genetic gain in the world.
