RealMe is an initiative from the New Zealand government and New Zealand Post to make doing things online easier and more secure.
The service provides the easiest and most secure way for New Zealanders, immigrants and organisations to verify their identity online. They exist to be at the centre of online identity in New Zealand, in both the public and private sectors.
