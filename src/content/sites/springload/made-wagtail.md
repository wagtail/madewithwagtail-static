Made with Wagtail is a showcase of sites and apps built on [Torchbox](https://torchbox.com/)'s Wagtail CMS.
Created by a team of passionate Springloaders during a [weekend hackathon](https://www.springload.co.nz/blog/made-wagtail-hackathon/), Made with Wagtail now features over 150 websites and apps from more than 100 developers around the world.
