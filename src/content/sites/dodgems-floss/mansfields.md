Dodgems and Floss started its partnership with Mansfields in 2019 with the team transforming their brand and digital presence.

**Refresh and modernisation**

Proud of their heritage as a respected family business, the team at Mansfields wanted to refresh the brand and website to showcase their scale and innovation, positioning them as a preferred partner for UK supermarket procurement teams.

**A website built to match Mansfields’ innovative ethos**

We built the website using Wagtail CMS, making it easy to update by the internal team whenever needed. Packed with engaging and unique features including; a swiper-powered fruit slider, subtle animations and a streamlined job application process to support overseas recruiting.

With a new user experience designed to specifically talk to both distributors and potential new staff, Mansfields stands out from other fruit producers. Ensuring they are serving and connecting with the right audience.

The site has given the business a new elevated look that supports their forward-focused growth plans.
