Prime Property Management were storming the property management market in London and the South East, but to keep monument for future growth they wanted to leverage the brand and showcase what makes the Prime Property Management offering different.

**A multi-site development**

The website was built with Wagtail CMS, which we recommend for its easy to use and intuitive interface. Our developers rate the Django framework for its robustness, security and scalability, while the content managers at Prime PM can add text and other assets simply in a couple of clicks. Both the main website, and the bespoke ‘My Prime Portal’ reside in the same Wagtail dashboard for simple, single sign-on services for everyone that needs to access it in the business.

A key feature the Prime PM team wanted to incorporate is their Trustpilot customer reviews, so we built this in using the API to feed reviews straight into the website. The reviews are shown in rotation, so there's new quotes from happy customers on your every visit.
