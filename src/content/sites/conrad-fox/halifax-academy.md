Simple, easy-to-use online ESL learning platform built with teachers and students in mind. The Halifax Academy platform uses Wagtail Streamfields to allow the quick creation of new dynamic exercises that simulate the active in-classroom experience. A testament to the simplicity of Wagtail itself, the Halifax Academy platform was designed by an ESL teacher, not a professional developer.
