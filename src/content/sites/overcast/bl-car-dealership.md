BL is one of Iceland’s largest and oldest automotive service companies, established in 1954. BL represents 12 car manufacturers, including BMW, MINI, Jaguar, Land Rover, Nissan, Renault, Dacia, Isuzu, Subaru, Hyundai, MG Motor, and operates Bílaland for used cars. Their services encompass the sale of new and used vehicles, maintenance and repair services, and spare parts supply.
