Mítra is a wholesale company that provides customers with quality tires.

Mitra.is features a license plate lookup to show customers a recommended tire size for their car and a calculator for changing tire sizes. When designing Mítra’s website, care was taken to ensure that it was simple, user-friendly, responsive for all devices, and would serve the Mítra brand well. We wanted to make it easy for customers to find information, use the calculators, and find the right tires for their needs.

Mítra’s website is made with Wagtail and has a connection to Business Central through an API where all product information and stock are fetched and updated in real time, even though products are purchased in other ways. When an order is placed, it automatically appears in Business Central.
