Garri has specialized in the import and sale of quality good and food packaging to professional chefs and clients in the food preparation industry in Iceland.

Their new website is an ambitious B2B e-commerce project with the goal of moving a substantial portion of their sales online. With a direct link to Microsoft Dynamics Ax, the whole product catalog, inventory and pricing structure can be presented and managed in Wagtail.

The frontend application is a combination of Wagtail and React.js - most of the marketing material is provided by standard Wagtail while the ecommerce application is written in React.js.

Design by ENNEMM.
