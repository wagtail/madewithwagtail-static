Sorpa is a waste management company in the Capital Region of Iceland. Sorpa's role is to manage waste, which includes operating a reception and sorting station, landfills, gas and composting stations, six recycling stations, as well as second-hand markets.

Sorpa's Wagtail website is focused on individuals, their needs, and providing customers with as much information about recycling as possible.

Sorpa’s Wagtail website features a map where users can see the locations of recycling stations and other information. If users are allowed to share their location, the website points to the nearest station. On the homepage, users can find a search engine where they can search for all kinds of waste and learn how to dispose of it.
