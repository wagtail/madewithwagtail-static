Búseti is a co-operative building association where the goal is to make good quality secure housing available to the members and manage it in a professional manner based on a long-term strategy with an emphasis on sustainability.

On Búseti´s Wagtail website users are able to register as members, apply for apartments and maintain everything about their subscription.
