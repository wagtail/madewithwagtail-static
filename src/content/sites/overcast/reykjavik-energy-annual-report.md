Annual report website for the local energy company Reykjavík Energy. This website is made out of multiple streamfield blocks, including modified table block and chartblocks. The mission was to create a reusable reporting tool that could be used for many years. The report also links the UN's Sustainable Development Goals to each chapter, showing how the company is conforming to the various goals.
