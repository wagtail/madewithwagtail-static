**Creation of a landscape architect website Jaulin Paysages.**

Following the buyout of the company by the Landais group, Snoweb was mandated to make their new logo as well as the redesign of the website.

In this project, Snoweb did :

- Reflection on the user experience: reduced access to information and ease of navigation on the website.
- Creation of the new identity and the web graphic charter : colors and images.
- Creation of the website pages, add text and image content, uploading and testing of the website.
