**Creation of the Weenect website, CMS and e-commerce.**

Weenect offer a complete range of connected GPS trackers for every member of your family : children, seniors, dogs and cats. Wherever you are, you'll always know where your relatives are too.

In this project, Snoweb did :

- Creation and development of several blocks with a custom theme to layout the website.
- Translation of the website in 6 languages.
- Management of payments and currencies (EUR / GBP / CHF) internationally: Paypal, Giropay, Sofort, iDeal, Bancontact, various credit cards
- Install automatic delivery system: don't manage your parcel shipments, the delivery company takes care of everything! Direct link with Crosslog or Salesupply.
