**Creation of the Well Office Coworking website.**

Well Office is a coworking place in Nantes, France.

In this project, Snoweb did :

- Reflection on the user experience: reduced access to information and ease of navigation on the website.
- Creation of the identity and the web graphic charter :  work on the choice of colors and images.
- Creation of the website pages, add text and image, uploading and testing of the website.
