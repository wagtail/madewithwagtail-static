**Creation of networking platform between freelancers and companies**

Station Spatiale aim is to connect a company with a freelance thanks to the freelance services.

That is the process :

1- Freelance registration

2- Freelance add just his services he likes to do

3- Company search in Station Spatiale catalog the service it need

4- Matching algorithm provides the profil which perfectly correspond to the company needs

5- Now they can communicate each other freely
