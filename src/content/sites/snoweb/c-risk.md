**Creation of the C-Risk website.**

C-Risk accompanies, advises and trains companies in the quantification of cyber risks.

In this project, Snoweb did :

- Reflection on the user experience: reduced access to information and ease of navigation on the website.
- Creation of the new identity and the web graphic charter: work on the choice of colors and images.
- Creation of the website pages, text and image content, uploading and testing of the website.
